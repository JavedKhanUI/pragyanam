About Us

Pragyanam is a new K-12 school in Gurugram, from the same group as Footprints Childcare; founded by IIT-IIM alumni. In the last six years, we have served over 12000 parents, across 80 centres in India. ‘Pragyanam’ which means wisdom or understanding in Sanskrit, has been founded on the core belief that the objective of learning in the 21st century is to raise mindful, conscientious individuals who root for competence over competitiveness and aspire to live a life of happiness and fulfillment. Pragyanam has developed a “No-Limit Curriculum” where subjects are used as a medium to develop life-skills. While the main focus is on developing these life-skills, learning will happen along the way.

Head Of School

An educationist since 1990; Ms. Geeta Verma has been the Founding Head of The International Program (International Baccalaureate[IB] & Cambridge ) at the Heritage Xperiential Learning School, Gurugram. Prior to Heritage, she was associated with Pathways group of schools as the IB Diploma Coordinator and Head of Department-English. She is an IB Diploma Programme Examiner and has led a number of authorizations and accreditations and also facilitated TAISI Workshops for School leaders and SAIBSA (South Asia International IB Schools Association) sessions in English Literature/ Extended Essay and Theory of Knowledge over the course of years. She is a graduate from the prestigious Lady Shri Ram College, Delhi University (D.U) and has done her B.Ed. on scholarship from the C.I.E [Central Institute of Education], Delhi University (DU). She has also completed a blended course- on pedagogical excellence from Stanford University.

The Founders

Raj Singhal

A father of twins and a seasoned entrepreneur Raj, is an alumnus of IIT Delhi. Amongst many of his laurels is his taking a software services firm to a 10 million turn over with 350 employees and 3 countries in 10 Years. He is passionate about transforming Indian Education Sector. As a Co-Founder and CEO of Footprints Childcare, he yearns towards creating world-class education facilities in India.

Ashish Aggarwal

Ashish is an alumnus of IIT Delhi and IIM Calcutta and the father of an 11-year old. He firmly believes that Indian Education System is long due for transformation and our schools need to play a significant role in creating mature and wise individuals who can happily take on any challenges life throws at them.

Purvesh Sharma

An IIT Delhi alumnus, Purvesh entered the field of education when he co-founded an e-Learning company in 2000. Purvesh is also a trained life coach and he focusses on helping people lead meaningful and fulfilling lives. His vision includes revolutionising education, by creating life ready children, who grow to be happy and satisfied adults.
